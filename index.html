Our Services

✻

We deal on Marine topcoat, Antifouling paints, Coat Tar black, Primers, Thinners and Zinc Phosphate Epoxy paints.

Coal Tar Black(pitch) (GP1100CB

Coal tar prevents rust formation and used as a base coat or under coat.

Antifouling paints(GP2013AF)

This is used for underwater hull paints and also to prevent microbial actions on the hull of the vessels.

Primers

It has excellent bonding capacity and produces good surface finish for undercoats for topcoats.

Epoxy TopCoats.

Finishing paints, applied after the primers as protective paints.

Zinc Phosphate Epoxy paint/primer (GP3018ZE)

Used as epoxy primers and sealers that give effect to non porous finish.It is recommended for power plants, water treatment plants and exteriors

Red oxide epoxy primer (GP1001PS)

Red oxide epoxy primer, is an anticorrosion coating, to inhibit rust formation and prevent metal oxidation.
